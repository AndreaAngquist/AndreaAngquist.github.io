Situation Room Remote Control

Situation Room is an Elementum product that lets customers create large scale video walls to display data from our apps. Designing situation room was unique since it's a true multi-deveice, multi-format product. The wall itself can be made up of a variable number of screens which need to be configured and managed. I designed a tablet remote control app- both a native iOS version and a responsive version - that lets our customers setup their own video wall and then reconfigure the wall as needed. Screens on the wall cycle through data and post updated data as we recieve it. As a result, I designed functionality for users to interact with screens on the wall via the remote control app, allowing them to pause screens when they see something compelling, or to fast forward and rewind.
