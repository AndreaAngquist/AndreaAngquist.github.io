About Me

I'm a user experience designer living in the Bay Area. Currently, I am working as a product and interaction designer at Elementum, focusing on design for analytics and Big Data. I design for web, iOS, Android and large screens. I practice both a user-centered and data-driven design process; users' needs are at the heart of what I design, and I use data to influence my design decisions.
